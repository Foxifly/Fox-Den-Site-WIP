About Fox Den | EDM

Fox Den is an imaginative plug.dj community built on creativity. You may remember us from MystiQommunity; now, we're back and better than ever to bring electronic dance music to your ears. We can assure you that everything created for Fox Den is custom-made for our enjoyment. Some of our features include: custom RCS room CSS, custom webpages, custom coded Node.js FoxBot, custom Discord server, welcoming and friendly staff, plug.dj events, and so much more.

We are a very close and loving community and we constantly are looking to expand. We care about each and every user that joins our Den and welcome them into any conversation. We are always wanting to chat and meet new people, so don't be shy! You may also catch us in Discord Voice laughing and having a great time, so feel free to make yourself at home. We're very excited you're interested in our community.

Here at Fox Den, we take pride in having the best staff members. We have adopted the friendliest, nicest, and most talented staff members to represent our community. Each of them are unique in their own way and bring so much to Fox Den. We would really be nothing without our amazing staff members; and we hope you think they're amazing too! Please mention them if you need help, advice, or even just someone to talk to. We're all here for you.
